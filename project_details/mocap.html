Blazepose: 3d pose estimation keypoints

Motion capture using Pose Estimation model2024

Our project utilizes state-of-the-art technology in motion capture and rendering high-quality 3D human models to produce animations, movies, and more. We leverage Mediapipe Blazepose, an advanced pose estimation model, to extract key pose estimations from videos or real-time camera feeds. Through the process of motion capture, we precisely capture the movements and gestures of human subjects, translating them into digital representations. By utilizing Mediapipe Blazepose, we accurately detect and extract key points on the human body. Once the pose estimation data is obtained, we connect pose data with 3D human models. Our ultimate goal is to create animations, movies, and other visual media by seamlessly integrating motion capture and rendering technologies. Whether it's bringing characters to life in animated films or creating realistic virtual environments, our project aims to push the boundaries of visual storytelling. We believe that our innovative approach will be helpful and save cost and time in producing animations, cartoons, movies, and other visual medias.
