Self-Ordering Applications.2019-2020

Self-ordering applications were implemented for KIOSK and Android devices. Cafe or Restaurant customers can order food or drinks by themself using Self-Ordering Applications Self-Ordering Applications are GUI Applications. To develop Self-Ordering Applications, the Openframeworks library is used with C++. Self-ordering applications were connected with Deep Learning. Face Detection, Face, Emotion, Age, and Gender Recognition are Deep Learning parts of Self-Ordering Applications. I have implemented 3 kinds of Self-Ordering Applications. Please watch Videos 1-3 to see the working process of Self-Ordering Applications.

  • Stages:
    • GUI implementation.
    • Face Identification
    • Age, Emotion and Gender Recognition
    • Face or Card Payment
    • etc.

  • Platform: Ubuntu, Windows, Android
  • Stack: Tensorflow, OpenCV, Openframeworks, Facenet.
